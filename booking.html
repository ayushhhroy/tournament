How Battle Royal Works

• Entry fee: ₹10 per player

• Each kill earns ₹10 (after 20% commission)

• 20% commission is deducted for tournament organization

• Example: With 5 kills, you earn ₹40 (₹50 - 20% commission)

Kills Prize Money Commission (20%) Total Earnings
1 kill ₹10 ₹2 ₹8
3 kills ₹30 ₹6 ₹24
5 kills ₹50 ₹10 ₹40
7 kills ₹70 ₹14 ₹56
10 kills ₹100 ₹20 ₹80

Note: The winning amount is calculated based on your kills with ₹10 per kill after 20% commission deduction.

How Clash Squad Works

• Single match - Winner (player or team) takes the entire prize

• 20% commission is deducted for tournament organization

• Winner receives 80% of the total entry fees collected

• Example: With ₹20 entry fee, winner receives ₹36 (after 20% commission)

Entry Fee Winning Amount Commission (20%)

₹500 ₹900 ₹100

Note: The winning amount is calculated based on a single match with one winner taking the entire prize after 20% commission.
